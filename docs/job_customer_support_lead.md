# Job Description — Customer Support Lead

**Job ID:** JOB-002
**Role title:** Customer Support Lead
**Department:** Customer Operations
**Match keywords:** customer support, support lead, team lead, escalation, CSAT, contact center, service

## Role summary
We are hiring a Customer Support Lead to manage a team of support agents,
own escalation handling, drive CSAT improvements, and partner with product
on recurring customer issues.

## Required qualifications
- 4+ years in customer support, with 1+ year leading a team
- Track record of improving CSAT or reducing resolution time
- Strong de-escalation and conflict-resolution skills
- Experience with ticketing systems (Zendesk, Freshdesk, or similar)
- Comfortable using data to coach agents and report to leadership

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in a regulated or healthcare-adjacent support environment
- Process documentation and SOP creation
- Workforce scheduling / capacity planning exposure

## Evaluation rubric (for the interview evaluator)
Score each dimension 1–5:
1. People leadership and coaching ability
2. De-escalation and customer-handling judgment
3. Data-driven decision making
4. Communication clarity
5. Process and operational thinking
A strong hire scores 4+ on dimensions 1, 2, and 4.
